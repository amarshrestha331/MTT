Our Story

Direct from the Source

At Mountain Tea Trade, we believe in bringing you the finest teas directly from their source. Our owner, Ram, regularly visits tea plantations in the Ilam region of Nepal to personally select the highest quality tea leaves and ensure fair trade practices.

With over 29 years of experience in the tea industry, we've built strong relationships with local farmers, allowing us to offer you exceptional teas at reasonable prices while supporting sustainable farming practices.

Our Premium Teas

Discover our selection of high-quality teas from the Himalayan highlands

Ilam Black Tea

Grown at 1300m altitude in the Ilam region of Nepal, this premium black tea offers a complex flavor profile with floral notes and a smooth finish. Similar to Darjeeling but with its own unique character.

Himalayan Green Tea

Our emerald green tea from the high mountains of Nepal is rich in antioxidants with a fresh, grassy flavor and subtle sweet notes. Perfect for daily drinking and health benefits.

Specialty Herbal Teas

Explore our collection of herbal infusions including Chamomile, Tulsi, and Rosemary. Sourced from small farms in Nepal, these caffeine-free options offer unique flavors and wellness benefits.
