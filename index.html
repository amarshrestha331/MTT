Our Story

Direct from the Source

At Mountain Tea Trade, we believe in bringing you the finest teas directly from their source. Our owner, Ram, regularly visits tea plantations in the Ilam region of Nepal to personally select the highest quality tea leaves and ensure fair trade practices.

With over 29 years of experience in the tea industry, we've built strong relationships with local farmers, allowing us to offer you exceptional teas at reasonable prices while supporting sustainable farming practices.

Silver Tea

Silver Needle Tea

Golden Needle Tea

Jasmine Tea

A green tea infused with jasmine blossoms, offering a naturally sweet and fragrant brew with calming effects.

Jasmine Flower Tea

Caffeine-free infusion of whole jasmine flowers, light and delicately sweet with a soothing floral fragrance.

Chamomile Tea

Golden infusion with apple-like notes made from dried chamomile flowers. Known for its calming and sleep-aiding properties.

Herbal Tea

Mint Tea

Ginger Tea

Masala Tea

Spices Tea

Ruby Tea

Deep red in color with a fruity and slightly tangy profile. A bold and antioxidant-rich tea made from wild or cultivated plants.
